FlirtMax privacy.

Quick overview

We collect information you provide, information created when you use FlirtMax, and basic technical and analytics information. We use that information to run the app, personalize your AI partner and coaching experience, keep track of streaks and progress, process subscriptions, improve safety and quality, and understand how people use the product.

Some of the information you choose to provide, such as dating goals, flirting preferences, relationship status, blockers, and conversation content, may be personal or sensitive depending on where you live. Please only share what you are comfortable having processed by FlirtMax and its service providers.

FlirtMax uses AI systems and service providers to generate conversational responses, coaching feedback, and avatar-related content. AI-generated messages are not from real people and should not be treated as professional, medical, legal, mental health, or relationship advice.

Information we collect

Account and profile information Name or nickname, age confirmation, sign-in details, user ID, preferences, onboarding answers, and app settings.
Personalization information Dating goals, flirting experience, blockers, interests, personality preferences, daily cadence, avatar style, and AI partner vibe selections.
Conversation content Messages, prompts, replies, coaching feedback, conversation history, and related metadata such as timestamps. If you use voice features, we may process audio input, transcripts, and generated voice responses.
Avatar and media information Avatar choices, generated avatar assets, style preferences, and prompts needed to generate or display AI avatar visuals.
Progress and rewards Streaks, levels, coins, completed sessions, reminders, and other app activity used to power your progress.
Usage, device, and analytics information Taps, clicks, screen views, feature usage, device type, operating system, app version, approximate region, crash data, diagnostics, and identifiers used to connect analytics to your account. Analytics events may be linked to your FlirtMax user ID so we can understand product usage and support your account.
Payment and subscription information Subscription status, paywall interactions, purchase confirmations, renewal state, and related transaction metadata. We do not collect or store your full payment card number.

How we use information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and secure FlirtMax.
  • Create and personalize your AI partner, conversations, coaching tone, and greetings.
  • Generate avatar visuals and related app assets based on your selected style and vibe.
  • Track streaks, levels, coins, daily practice, reminders, and other progress features.
  • Analyze app usage, taps, clicks, paywall performance, onboarding flow, and feature quality.
  • Process subscriptions, purchases, refunds, trials, and access to paid features.
  • Detect abuse, enforce our rules, troubleshoot issues, and protect users and the app.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

How we use AI

FlirtMax is built around AI-powered coaching. Your onboarding answers, preferences, messages, conversation history, and relevant app activity may be sent to AI models or AI infrastructure providers so they can generate responses, roleplay scenarios, coaching feedback, summaries, safety checks, and avatar-related outputs.

AI output can be inaccurate, unexpected, or incomplete. You are responsible for how you use the app and how you communicate with real people outside the app. Do not submit information you do not want processed by AI systems, including highly sensitive personal information, financial information, government identifiers, passwords, or private information about other people without permission.

We may use conversation and usage data to monitor quality, improve prompts and product behavior, debug issues, measure safety, and evaluate model performance. Where possible, we limit access to people and service providers who need it to operate or improve FlirtMax.

How we share information

We do not sell your conversation content or use it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us provide hosting, authentication, database, analytics, AI processing, avatar generation, subscriptions, paywalls, customer support, security, and diagnostics. These may include providers such as Supabase, Mixpanel, Superwall, Higgsfield, AI model providers, and similar infrastructure vendors.
  • AI and avatar providers that process prompts, messages, preferences, or related data to generate coach responses, safety outputs, transcripts, voice responses, or avatar visuals.
  • Payment and platform providers such as Apple, App Store services, and subscription infrastructure used to process purchases and manage access.
  • Legal, safety, and business purposes when required to comply with law, enforce our terms, protect rights and safety, prevent fraud or abuse, or as part of a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Adults only

FlirtMax is intended only for people who are 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that a minor has created an account or provided personal information, we will take reasonable steps to delete that information and close the account.

If you believe a minor has used FlirtMax or provided personal information, contact us using the information below.

Your choices

  • You can update certain profile, onboarding, and reminder preferences in the app.
  • You can stop submitting conversation content at any time by not using chat or voice features.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your App Store account settings.
  • You can request access, correction, deletion, or export of your personal information.
  • You can request that we close your account and delete personal information, subject to legal, safety, fraud-prevention, and operational retention needs.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights, including rights to know, access, correct, delete, port, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information. We will honor applicable rights requests as required by law.

Data retention

We keep personal information for as long as needed to provide FlirtMax, maintain your account, personalize your AI experience, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent abuse, and improve product safety and reliability. Conversation history, analytics records, purchase records, and backup copies may have different retention periods.

When we no longer need information, we will delete it, anonymize it, or take reasonable steps to prevent it from being associated with you, unless we are required or permitted to keep it.

Security and transfers

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No internet or mobile app service can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so please use care when deciding what to share.

FlirtMax and its service providers may process information in the United States and other countries. Those countries may have privacy laws different from where you live.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and provide additional notice when required by law. Your continued use of FlirtMax after an update means the updated policy applies to future use of the app.
